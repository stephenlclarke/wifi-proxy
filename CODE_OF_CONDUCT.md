# Code Of Conduct

This project expects respectful, focused, and welcoming collaboration in issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and related community spaces.

Contributors should:

- Be kind and constructive when giving feedback.
- Assume good intent while still asking precise technical questions.
- Keep discussions focused on the work.
- Avoid harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, or publishing private information.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that do not meet these expectations. Repeated or severe violations may result in blocked participation in the project.
